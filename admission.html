What are the requirements?

Potential future students should be at least 17 years old and older. Applicants must have a passion for magic or born talent with the magic.
Being from the magic related family is an asset. Knowledge of English is a must as if the lectures are in English. Ability to be ready to meet magical animals.

Documents needed for application

  • Proof of Identification(passport, government id) in English(must be translated)
  • English test IELTS with the minimum grade 6.0
  • Parents' or Caregivers' Full name, address, phone number, social status, education level, magic talents if applicable
  • Demonstation of the passion for magic by a video recording of 2 minutes
  • Health Information Card
  • High School Degree
