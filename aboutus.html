HOGWARTS SUBJECTS

Core Classes

  • Astronomy

    Study the stars, planets, and celestial phenomena in this magical discipline. Students learn about the movements of celestial bodies and their impact on magic.

  • Charms

    Master the art of casting spells with precision and finesse. Charms encompass a wide range of magical abilities, from summoning objects to charming creatures.

  • Defence Against the Dark Arts

    Equip students with practical skills to defend against dark magic and creatures, emphasizing both offensive and defensive techniques.

  • Herbology

    Study magical plants and fungi, learning their properties and uses in potion-making and magical creatures care.

  • History of Magic

    Explore the magical history of the wizarding world, including significant events, famous wizards, and the evolution of magical practices.

  • Potions

    Master the art of brewing magical concoctions and elixirs, understanding the properties and interactions of various magical ingredients.

  • Transfiguration

    Learn the art of changing the form or appearance of an object, mastering the complex and versatile magical skill of transfiguration.

Elective Classes

  • Alchemy

    Explore the magical properties of substances and the transformation of matter. Alchemy is a blend of science and magic, aiming to create powerful potions and elixirs.

  • Arithmancy

    Delve into the magical properties of numbers. Arithmancy involves the study of magical equations and the use of numbers to predict future events.

  • Care of Magical Creatures

    Learn the care and handling of magical creatures, from Hippogriffs to Blast-Ended Skrewts, emphasizing both practical knowledge and magical sensitivity.

  • Divination

    Explore the mystical art of predicting the future and interpreting signs and omens through various magical methods and tools.

  • Muggle Studies

    Study the lifestyle, culture, and technology of non-magical people (Muggles), fostering an understanding and appreciation of the Muggle world.
