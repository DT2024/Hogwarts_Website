TUITION & FINANCIAL AID

TUITION AT HOGWARTS IS FREE FOR ALL STUDENTS.

  • Education at Hogwarts is a right for every young witch or wizard in Britain.

  • While tuition might be covered, students still need to purchase their own books, robes, wands, and other supplies.Financial aid available for students who can't afford their school supplies.

  • Please do read take a moment to carefully review the following criteria to determine if you are eligible for financial assistance.

Please find Professor McGonagall in her office to fill the application form if you need aids. Be aware of the cat!

AID FROM HUFFLEPUFF

Every orphan is eligible for the Hufflepuff's fundamental financial aid, designed to support the acquisition of essential academic tools, including wands, wizarding robes, owls, and the like.

AID FROM GRYFFINDOR

Student demonstrating aptitude in flying and Quidditch will be granted a monthly tuition subsidy of 493 Knuts by Gryffindor House.

AID FROM SLYTHERIN

Students excelling in Potion-making are eligible to apply for the Slytherin House's Potion-making Scholarship, contingent upon achieving a grade of A+ in the subject.

AID FROM RAVENCLAW

Students who serve as assistants to professors will be remunerated from Ravenclaw based on their hours of service, at a rate no less than 14 Knuts per hour.
