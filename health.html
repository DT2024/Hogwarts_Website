About Our Health Center

Welcome to the Hogwarts University Health Center. We are dedicated to providing quality healthcare services to our students, faculty, and staff. Whenever you feel unwell or sick physically or mentally, feel free to reach out our team. Hogwarts University Hospitals works 24/7 even during public holidays such as Christmas. Our students, faculty and staff all are covered by Hogwarts Insurance, as a result, do not hesitate to contact us or pay visit Hogwarts Hospital or Health Center located on the 2nd floor of the main building.

Our Services

  • Primary Care
  • Mental Health Counseling
  • Immunizations
  • Health and Magic Education
  • Wizards and Witch vaccinations
  • Local University Hospital stay
