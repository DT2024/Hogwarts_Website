Hogwarts Library Regulations

Silence is Golden: All students are expected to maintain a quiet atmosphere. Whispering spells may be cast if absolutely necessary.

  • The Restricted Section houses books on advanced magic and potentially dangerous spells. A written note from a professor is required for access.

  • Magical books can be temperamental. Handle with care. Some books might try to bite or fly away.

  • All borrowed items must be returned within two weeks. Overdue books might hex the procrastinator.

  • Quills with self-inking or auto-correcting charms are not allowed. Invisible ink is strictly forbidden.

  • Absolutely no food or beverages are permitted. Accidental spills might lead to unintended magical reactions.

  • Repairing damaged books isn't as simple as casting a spell. Students will be held responsible for any harm they cause.

Beware!!!
Some of the books bite.

Restricted Section: This section is only accessible with special permission (usually requires a signed note from a professor). It contains books that are considered too dangerous or advanced for students.

Study Tables:There are numerous study tables and chairs spread throughout the library. Some are placed in quiet corners for those who prefer solitude.

The Grey Lady:Beware of the library ghost, She is usually helpful and might guide you to a book you're searching for.

Madam Irma Pince: She is the Hogwarts librarian. Always approach her with respect and care. She is very protective of the books and will not hesitate to give out penalties for mishandling.
